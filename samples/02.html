Research

ETH Zurich carries out fundamental research to broaden the knowledge base and provide the starting point for future innovative applications.

ETH's research is focused on the needs of society – be that at local, national or global level – and so makes a valuable contribution to the economy, politics and society in general.

Its main thematic focus areas are the world food system, cities of the future, climate change, energy, health, risk research, information processing, new materials and industrial processes.
